Airline Passenger Time Series Analysis

Overview:
The dataset contains monthly airline passenger counts from 1949 to 1960.

Key Findings:

• Passenger numbers show a strong upward trend.
• Clear seasonal pattern occurs every year.
• Moving averages highlight long-term growth.
• Forecast predicts continued passenger increase.

Insights:
Air travel demand is steadily growing with predictable seasonal peaks, helping airlines plan capacity and pricing strategies.
